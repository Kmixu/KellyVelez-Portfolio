My Life in Moments

Hello!

My name is Kelly Velez Galvez. I'm a mom, a full-time student at Valencia College, and someone who thrives on creativity and connection. I'm currently pursuing a degree in Graphic and Interactive Design because I love visual storytelling and bringing ideas to life through design.

Where I Work

I work at Epic Universe in the Ministry of Magic area as an Operations Attendant. My role involves ensuring smooth attraction operations and creating memorable, welcoming experiences for all guests. From managing guest flow and safety to providing directions and delivering genuine hospitality, I take pride in helping visitors feel immersed and excited in their magical journey.

Working here isn't just a job—it's a chance to be part of something larger than myself, a world-class team delivering joy and wonder every day. I'm proud to help turn a visit into an unforgettable adventure.

Where I'm From

I'm originally from Ecuador, a country full of culture, tradition, and strong family values. I moved to the U.S. when I was 14, which was both exciting and challenging. Starting a new life in a different country helped me grow in resilience and adaptability.

Learning English, adjusting to a new environment, and finding my place taught me to work hard and appreciate diversity. Today, I'm proud of how far I've come—from those early days to now studying and working in a creative field I love.
